Australian Astronomical Optics (AAO) is a recognised world leader in innovative astronomical instrumentation, software and research.
Thunder Down Under had the opportunity to visit the AAO to demonstrate the happenings within the team as of 2024, including our robot Baakan.
Students from our team had the opportunity to tour around the building and observe the technology being created by the AAO, and speak with other staff from the AAO.
