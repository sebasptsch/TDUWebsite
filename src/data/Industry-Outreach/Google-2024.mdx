With some of our mentors working at Google and as one of our sponsors, Thunder Down Under had the opportunity to visit Google Headquarters in Sydney to demonstrate the happenings within the team as of 2024, including our robot Baakan.
Students from our team had the opportunity to tour around the headquarters and speak with other employees within Google.
